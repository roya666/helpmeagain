Personalized technology education brought to you.

Klari Learning is a service that provides customized sessions for understanding your everyday tech devices. We connect you to trained coaches via Zoom for regular sessions and offer follow up reference resources after each meeting. Klari Learning's focus is collaboration, memory retention, and empowerment.

How It Works

Klari Learning fits to your schedule and offers sessions at a pace that is best for you.

Get Started

Submit your information and let us know what you are interested in learning about. Klari Learning will connect you to a coach.

Learn

You and your coach will collaborate using Zoom to answer your tech use questions.

Daily Use

Klari Learning keeps track of your progress and provides you with follow-up resources to reference as you implement your learning into your daily tech use.

“We have been frustrated and felt inept by past computer trainings which were too fast paced, dense, and overwhelming for us. Our experience with Klari has been much more helpful and personalized.

We identify skills that we want and need to learn, such as sending email attachments or organizing our file. In each session we address 2 or 3 tasks and are able to work at a pace comfortable to us and practice the skill with someone there to prompt us. After, we get an outline of what we've learned to refer to if we need to review.

Our coach is patient and goes at a pace we can handle. We are left feeling excited and empowered as a result of each session. We have found that we can now really use the new skills we have worked on. ”

Barbara and Alan, age 74 and 79
